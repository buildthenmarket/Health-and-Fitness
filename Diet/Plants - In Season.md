# Seasonal

- apricots and asparagus in the **spring**,
- blackberries and blueberries in the **summer**,
- parsnips and pears in the **fall and winter.**
- apples, bananas, carrots and celery **all year long**

Dragon fruit: White dragon fruit is typically in season during the summer months, specifically from June through September Yellow dragon fruit, on the other hand, has a longer ripening period and can be harvested from September to May However, the specific peak season for yellow dragon fruit can vary depending on the region and growing conditions. In some regions, such as South America, the peak season for dragon fruit, including yellow varieties, is between December and March
